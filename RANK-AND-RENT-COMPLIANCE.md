# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

This document outlines the compliance requirements for content on this window and door replacement rank and rent site.

## Core Principles

### 1. No False Claims About Past Work

- Never claim specific past projects or clients
- Do not fabricate project timelines or outcomes
- Do not create fake case studies or project histories
- Frame all scenarios as "examples of the type of engagement we can handle"

### 2. Transparent Representation

- Clearly identify that we connect property owners with contractors
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Be honest about our role in the contractor matching process
- Do not misrepresent the nature of the business

### 3. No Unverifiable Social Proof

- No testimonials without verified sources
- No review stars or ratings without legitimate backing
- No industry badges or certifications unless properly sourced
- No awards or recognition claims without verification

### 4. Educational Content Only

- Provide educational information about windows and doors
- Not engineering advice - defer to licensed professionals
- Not legal advice - direct to attorneys for legal questions
- Not construction advice - recommend consulting licensed contractors
- Always include appropriate disclaimers

### 5. Verification Responsibility

- Users should verify contractor licenses before hiring
- Users should confirm insurance coverage with contractors
- Users should check references and credentials independently
- Users should obtain multiple quotes for projects

## Content Requirements

### Service Pages

- Describe what the service includes
- Explain who the service is for
- Provide general timeline expectations (not guarantees)
- Include local permit and code information where relevant
- Frame capabilities as "types of engagements we can handle"
- Include disclaimer about educational content

### Location Pages

- Provide accurate geographic and demographic information
- Mention local construction activity and trends
- List relevant building codes and permit resources
- Do not fabricate local project claims
- Do not invent specific client relationships

### Brand Pages

- Provide factual information about manufacturers
- Include accurate product descriptions and features
- Do not claim exclusive relationships without authorization
- Do not misrepresent warranty or product capabilities

### Blog Content

- Focus on educational topics
- Provide general guidance, not specific recommendations
- Include disclaimers about professional consultation
- Link to authoritative sources for technical information

## Required Disclaimers

### Footer Disclosure

All pages must include this footer disclosure:

```
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for window and door contractor matching and project coordination support.
```

### Educational Content Disclaimer

Service and location pages must include:

```
Educational content only. Not engineering, legal, or construction advice. Consult licensed professionals for specific project guidance.
```

### Example Capability Disclaimer

When describing project capabilities, include:

```
"Example of the type of engagement we can handle. Contact us to discuss your specific project in [CITY], [STATE]. We can share references upon request."
```

## What to Avoid

- Do not fabricate years in business or experience claims
- Do not create fake employee profiles or team photos
- Do not claim specific certifications without proof
- Do not guarantee outcomes or timelines
- Do not use "we installed" language for specific past projects
- Do not create fictitious customer testimonials
- Do not claim awards or recognition without verification

## What to Include

- General information about window and door types
- Educational content about energy efficiency
- Local building code and permit resources
- Industry standards and best practices
- Manufacturer product information
- Service area coverage details
- Contact forms for inquiry routing

## User Protection

- Always encourage users to verify contractor credentials
- Recommend obtaining multiple quotes
- Advise checking licenses and insurance
- Suggest asking for and checking references
- Promote informed decision-making
- Provide links to official licensing and permit resources

## Compliance Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:

- [ ] No specific past project claims
- [ ] No fabricated testimonials or reviews
- [ ] No unverifiable experience or credential claims
- [ ] Educational disclaimers included
- [ ] Footer disclosure present
- [ ] Local resource links accurate
- [ ] Example capabilities properly framed
- [ ] No legal or engineering advice
- [ ] Contractor verification encouraged
- [ ] Honest representation of business model

## Questions or Concerns

If you are unsure whether content complies with these guidelines, err on the side of caution and:

1. Remove specific claims about past work
2. Add appropriate disclaimers
3. Frame as educational information
4. Encourage professional consultation
5. Be transparent about the business model

Rank and rent success depends on honest representation and valuable educational content that helps property owners make informed decisions about their window and door projects.
